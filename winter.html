Winter Activities Information

Winter is magically romantic with a crackling wood fire, a glass of champagne and your own bubbling Whirlpool Bath Tub and private rejuvenating Hot Tub, in-room real crackling wood fireplace and with romantic 1st Class gourmet dining.
  Picture perfect with pure white snow covered trees and lakes sparkling like diamonds in the snow. Enjoy a surreal evening of walking under the most brilliant stars.  For those who need a little help getting outdoors we even have all the outdoor clothing for you if you decide to participate.

ALGONQUIN VISITOR CENTER

Pristine and natural snow covered Algonquin Park is home to world-class venues.
Algonquin Park's Visitor Centre is open daily, has exhibits on the Park's natural and human history, a relaxing restaurant, an excellent bookstore, theatre and a viewing deck of the Winter wild Algonquin landscape.
ALGONQUIN PARK LIVE NOW WEATHER CAM
A free Park Pass is available for you from our Office.

Free Available Gear

  • IceHiking Gear: Crampons and ski poles for walking trails from November to April if there is ice or snow on the Algonquin Park Trails.
  • Cross Country Ski Gear: Wax-less cross-country skis, boots and poles.
  • Snowshoe Gear: Pro snowshoes, waxing station for those that enjoy waxing. Waxing station for those that enjoy waxing.
  • Ice Skate Gear: Ice Skates up to size 14. Ask us about the Mew Lake Park Pass from our Office
  • Clothing: Winter boots, Coats, Gloves. 100% windproof & waterproof pants and jackets plus polar fleece interiors, if needed.

Snowmobiling Outside of Couples Resort

Bring your own Snowmobile, the main trail system is 1 km away and easy to sled to.
